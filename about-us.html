Our Services

Unlock the power of precision with PRECISION CALIBRATION & REPAIR. Our cutting-edge calibration service delivers invaluable insights into your equipment's tolerance levels, all at an incredibly affordable price point.

Rest assured, we are fully compliant with industry-leading standards, including ISO/IEC 9001, ISO/IEC 17025, ANSI Z540, ISO 10012-1, and MIL-STD 45662A. As trailblazers in the field, we ensure that all our primary standards are traceable to the esteemed National Institute of Standards and Technology, NIST (formerly NBS).

At PRECISION CALIBRATION & REPAIR, we stay ahead of the game by adhering to the latest revisions of codes, standards, and specifications. This unwavering commitment to excellence guarantees a standardized methodology, resulting in the highest quality products and services for you.Say goodbye to unnecessary costs, as we provide a comprehensive certification database and automated Recall system, completely free of charge. With a swift turnaround time of 5 to 7 working days, our Calibration Services keep your operations running seamlessly.

As part of our dedication to superior service, we even offer minor cleaning of tools and cases at no additional cost. Additionally, our services extend to small parts certification, ensuring a comprehensive approach to your calibration needs.

Experience the convenience of our climate-controlled shipping, receiving, and order processing area. Your equipment will be handled with the utmost care, maintaining optimal conditions throughout its journey.

Join the PRECISION CALIBRATION & REPAIR revolution today, and unlock a world of precision, efficiency, and unmatched quality.

Our Services

Elevate your instrument repair experience with Precision Calibration & Repair. Our dedicated team specializes in repairing inspection and measuring tools, as well as assembly tools, ensuring that your equipment is restored to peak performance.

With an impressive collective experience of over 75 years, our sales and service staff is committed to providing you with efficient and cost-effective solutions for inspection tool maintenance and certification. Trust us to deliver results that save you time and money without compromising on quality.
At Precision Calibration & Repair, we excel in repairing a wide range of tools, including Dial and Test Indicators, Calipers, Bore Gages, Micrometers, Torque Wrenches, Height Gages, and more. Rest assured that our skilled technicians are well-versed in handling top brands like Starrett, Mitutoyo, and Brown & Sharpe, with a comprehensive inventory of common parts readily available.

When you choose Precision Calibration & Repair, you benefit from our ability to minimize downtime and keep your operations running smoothly. We understand the importance of prompt repairs, and our extensive stock of parts ensures a swift turnaround time, saving you valuable resources.
Moreover, we possess the advanced equipment and expertise necessary to flawlessly lap virtually any micrometer or caliper. This precision process guarantees the restoration of optimal performance, allowing you to rely on your equipment with complete confidence.

Experience the difference of Precision Calibration & Repair's instrument repair services. Trust us to breathe new life into your tools, enabling you to maintain peak productivity and accuracy.
